core.epanet.hydraulics.node.Junction.emitter_coefficient

Emitters are used to model flow through sprinkler heads or pipe leaks.

Flow out of the emitter equals the product of the flow coefficient and the junction pressure raised to EMITTER EXPONENT, which defaults to 0.5 and can be set in OPTIONS section.
