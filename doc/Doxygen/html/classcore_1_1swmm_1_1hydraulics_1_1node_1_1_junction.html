◆ ponded_area

core.swmm.hydraulics.node.Junction.ponded_area

Area occupied by ponded water atop the junction after flooding occurs (sq.

feet or sq. meters). If the Allow Ponding simulation option is turned on, a non-zero value of this parameter will allow ponded water to be stored and subsequently returned to the conveyance system when capacity exists. (Apond)

◆ surcharge_depth

core.swmm.hydraulics.node.Junction.surcharge_depth

Additional depth of water beyond the maximum depth that is allowed before the junction floods (feet or meters).

This parameter can be used to simulate bolted manhole covers or force main connections. (Ysur)
