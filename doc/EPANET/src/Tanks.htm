Tanks

Tanks are nodes with storage capacity, where the volume of stored water can vary with time during a simulation.

     The primary input properties for tanks are:

     bottom elevation

     diameter (or shape if non-cylindrical)

     initial, minimum and maximum water levels

     initial water quality.

     The principal computed outputs are:

     total head (water surface elevation)

     water quality.

     Tanks are required to operate within their minimum and maximum levels. EPANET stops outflow if a tank is at its minimum level and stops inflow if it is at its maximum level.

     Tanks can also serve as water quality source points.
