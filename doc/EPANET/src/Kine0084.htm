Kinetic Models - First-Order Saturation Growth

R = Kb (CL - C)

This model can be applied to the growth of disinfection by-products, such as trihalomethanes, where the ultimate formation of by-product (CL) is limited by the amount of reactive precursor present. Bottle tests can be used to estimate Kb if the test is carried out long enough to measure CL directly. (Kb is the slope of the plot of log [(CL - Ct)/Co] versus time, where Ct is concentration after time t and Co is concentration at time 0.)
